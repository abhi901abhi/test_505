What is Server Virtualization?

In Computing World, It is a known fact that Physical Machines like laptops, Desktops and Physical Servers are installed with an Operating system Like Windows, UNIX/Linux or Mac. And further an Application on top of it. Technically this is referred to as a Standalone hosting.

In the Figure, Application layer communicates with the hosted operating system layer to get the required hardware resources to function. Best way to term it as 3-Layer communication Architecture


Let me make it very simple “how standalone hosting works” with a real time example.


Sayyou play a movie in VLC Player (Application) and the movie should be played with an audio.


  1. 1. VLC Player requests the operating system layer for the sound to transmit to the audio device.

  2. 2. The Operating system communicates with the Kernel (Heart of Operating System)

  3. 3. Whichin turn Kernel calls the audio driver to talk to the audio device.

  4. 4. And that’s when you hear the sound of your favorite movie 
